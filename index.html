My Process

01

Discovery

Clarifying the problem before touching the solution.
I work to understand the real constraints, goals, and user context—often uncovering what actually needs to be built, not just what was initially requested.

02

Envision

Turning direction into a tangible plan.
I map flows, interaction beats, and technical feasibility so the experience is coherent before we commit to implementation.

03

Build

Shipping the smallest useful version—fast.
I prototype and implement with performance, clarity, and real-world constraints in mind, iterating toward polish as signals emerge.

04

Launch

Making it real, then making it better.
I validate in the wild, collect feedback, and tune the system—improving the experience based on what people actually do, not what we assumed.
